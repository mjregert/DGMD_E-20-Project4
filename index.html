Snowman!

This game is similar to "Hangman" but with a snowman (more appropriate!). Enter in letters one at a time to guess the word. If the letter you pick is not in the word, a portion of the snowman is drawn. If the snowman is completely drawn before the word is guessed, you lose. The snowman consists of a "Bottom", "Middle", "Left Arm", "Right Arm", and "Head" which gives you 5 tries.
